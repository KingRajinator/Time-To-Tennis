Chapters

The Austin chapter aims to introduce tennis and nurture the love of the sport in young children of all backgrounds. Through our events and classes, we hope to make tennis, a sport which is often only enjoyed by those with wealth, accessible to all. Our programs foster sportsmanship and teach children the rewarding nature of playing a sport. TimeToTennis is an organization by the community, and for the community. We rely on donations and the generosity of those around us so that we can keep our programs low-cost and accessible to all people. Together, we can create an environment that promotes growth and enjoyment for all children that increases with every ball hit.

In the California chapter, we pride ourselves on providing accessible and affordable tennis education, to not only spread our love for the sport, but to also promote hard work and discipline among our players. Our main goal is to be able to positively influence as many local youth as possible through providing access to easily accessible tennis education. To help us achieve this, we will set up donation boxes at popular sporting locations to help collect funds and supplies that will go towards necessary equipment, in addition to providing extremely inexpensive private lessons with one of our many experienced, volunteer coaches. We also plan to host completely free community events in the near future to encourage widespread participation and unity within the community. From your Chapter Leads: Sohan Jayanth, Aryamann Marwaha, and Sarang Kashyap, we are looking forward to seeing you out on the courts!

At the Chicago chapter, we focus on inclusion and diversity. Our goal is to make sure that everyone has a chance to play tennis and will not be held back because of their social class or status. In order to help achieve this goal, we have set up donation boxes at very prestigious tennis facilities where people have the opportunity to help the community by donating their old equipment. In addition, we are also working on crafting free events open to the community so that everyone can play tennis if they want to. We are working to dismantle the exclusivity of tennis, a traditionally rich sport, and allow spaces for those who are underprivileged. Everything we do will be cost free and driven by our sole goal, to bring the community together, one stroke at a time.
