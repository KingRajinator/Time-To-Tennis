San Ramon, California

In the California chapter, we pride ourselves on providing accessible and affordable tennis education, to not only spread our love for the sport, but to also promote hard work and discipline among our players. Our main goal is to be able to positively influence as many local youth as possible through providing access to easily accessible tennis education. To help us achieve this, we will set up donation boxes at popular sporting locations to help collect funds and supplies that will go towards necessary equipment, in addition to providing extremely inexpensive private lessons with one of our many experienced, volunteer coaches. We also plan to host completely free community events in the near future to encourage widespread participation and unity within the community. From your Chapter Leads: Sohan Jayanth, Aryamann Marwaha, and Sarang Kashyap, we are looking forward to seeing you out on the courts!

Sohan Jayanth - Chapter Director

Sohan Jayanth is a rising senior at Dougherty Valley High School in San Ramon, California. He also loves to give back to the community by tutoring underprivileged kids for free and volunteering for organizations such as Interact and Rotary. Sohan joined Time To Tennis in hopes that he could further contribute to his community and work with local youth while also spreading his passion for the sport.

Sarang Jayanth - Chapter Director

Sarang Kashyap is a rising senior at Dougherty Valley High School in San Ramon, CA. He has been on the varsity tennis team since his freshman year and was co-captain for the team in his third year of high school. He is very passionate about tennis and has been playing the sport for over 8 years. He loves working with kids and is looking forward to helping kids improve their tennis skills. Sarang is excited to host events with the rest of the chapter directors and bring tennis to kids from all types of economic backgrounds.

Aryamann Marwaha- Chapter Director

Aryamann Marwaha is an incoming senior at Dougherty Valley High school in San Ramon California. He has been playing for the past 7 years, 5 of which he played competitively. He played #1 JV his freshman year, and played on the varsity level his next too. Aryamann has also played doubles sectionals in the boys 18s division as a 15 year old. He also has experience with teaching kids, and has taught kids in San Ramon for a year. He is a passionate and hard working coach who is dedicated to give back to his community

Time To Tennis is a national non-profit based in the greater Seattle area. We believe sports are important for kids to be a part of. Empowering the youth for lifetime success through free and accessible tennis resources and classes!
